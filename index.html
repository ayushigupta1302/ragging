ANTI-RAGGING AWARENESS

A Safe and Respectful Campus

Ragging is a serious issue that can have devastating consequences for students. This website is dedicated to raising awareness about anti-ragging measures, promoting a safe and respectful campus environment, and providing resources for students who have been affected by ragging.


























WHY ANTI-RAGGING INITIATIVES MATTER

ANTI-RAGGING INITIATIVES MATTER BECAUSE THEY PROTECT STUDENTS FROM HARMFUL AND DEGRADING PRACTICES, ENSURING A SAFE AND SUPPORTIVE ENVIRONMENT WHERE EVERYONE CAN THRIVE. BY PREVENTING RAGGING, THESE INITIATIVES FOSTER RESPECT, EMPATHY, AND INCLUSIVITY, WHICH ARE ESSENTIAL FOR THE WELL-BEING AND DEVELOPMENT OF STUDENTS ON CAMPUS.

LAWS AND REGULATIONS

UNDER THE ANTI-RAGGING LAW, IF A STUDENT IS FOUND GUILTY, HE/SHE CAN BE IMPRISONED FOR 3 YEARS ALONG WITH A FINE.
